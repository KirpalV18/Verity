§ 05 — FAQ

Answers to the
obvious questions.

How accurate is AI text detection?

+

No detector is perfect — and anyone claiming otherwise is misleading you. Our goal is a probabilistic confidence score with transparent reasoning, so you can make informed judgments rather than blindly trust a binary result.

Does Verity store the content I browse?

+

No. Privacy is a core design principle. Text analysis runs without logging or storing your browsing data. We built Verity to restore trust — it would be contradictory to betray yours.

Will it slow down my browser?

+

Performance is a first-class concern. Analysis runs asynchronously with negligible impact on page load and scroll. You shouldn't notice it's there.

When will the extension launch?

+

We're in active development. Join the waitlist and you'll be first to know — we'll only reach out when it's genuinely ready. Quality over announcements.

Is Verity free to use?

+

The early access version will be free. We're focused on validating the product before thinking about pricing. Early waitlist members receive the best possible terms.

Which browsers are supported?

+

Phase 1 targets Chrome and Chromium-based browsers (Edge, Brave). Firefox support is planned based on Phase 1 feedback and demand signals from the waitlist.
